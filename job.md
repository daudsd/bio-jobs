## Work Permit — What Zarqa Needs

- Norway is **not EU** but is **EEA** — requires a **skilled worker permit** (not EU Blue Card)
- Needs a **job offer first**, then applies to UDI: [udi.no/en/want-to-apply/work-immigration/skilled-workers](https://www.udi.no/en/want-to-apply/work-immigration/skilled-workers/)
- MSc from University of Iceland qualifies as skilled worker
- Processing time: ~1–3 months — apply as soon as offer is received
- Iceland → Norway move is straightforward within EEA free movement rules
